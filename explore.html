Ferry Wheel

Experience the thrill of soaring high above the city on our magnificent Ferris wheel! With breathtaking views and a gentle, relaxing ride, our Ferris wheel is the perfect attraction for all ages. Step into one of our comfortable gondolas and let the world fade away as you rise higher and higher into the sky. Don’t miss out on this unforgettable experience - come ride our Ferris wheel today!

Roller Coaster

Get ready for the ride of your life on our exhilarating roller coaster! Feel the wind in your hair and the adrenaline pumping through your veins as you twist, turn, and plummet at breakneck speeds. Our state-of-the-art coaster is designed for maximum thrills, with heart-stopping drops and breathtaking loops that will leave you screaming for more. Don’t miss out on the ultimate thrill ride - come experience our roller coaster today!

MerryGoRound

Step back in time and experience the magic of our enchanting Merry Go Round! With its beautifully painted horses and charming music, our carousel is a timeless attraction that will delight both young and old. Take a spin on one of our majestic steeds and feel the joy of simpler times as you go round and round. Don’t miss out on this classic ride - come experience the charm of our Merry Go Round today

Umbrella

Escape the heat and enjoy a leisurely ride on our delightful umbrella ride! With its colorful umbrellas and gentle motion, our ride is the perfect way to relax and unwind. Sit back and enjoy the breeze as you glide through the air, taking in the sights and sounds of the park. Perfect for all ages, our umbrella ride is a must-try attraction. Don’t miss out - come experience the charm of our umbrella ride today!
